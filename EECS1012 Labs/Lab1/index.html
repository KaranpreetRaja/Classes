Welcome to the Web Sandbox!

This host is running an Apache web server. You can create files in the www directory of your home directory, and connect to them in one of two ways:

From inside the host, start Firefox Web Browser, and click the "Web Sandbox" shortcut, or, visit: http://localhost

From outside the host (eg. from your mobile phone or your laptop), follow these steps:

  • On the EECS lab workstation, open a Terminal (right-click the desktop, and choose "Open Terminal"), and type the command: "hostname". This will return the hostname of the computer (eg. "myhost"). You may also find the hostname located on a sticker on the machine.
  • Open a web browser on your device, and visit http://HOSTNAME.eecs.yorku.ca where HOSTNAME is the name returned by the hostname command above.
If you need assistance, please consult the lab technician, course TA, or your professor.
